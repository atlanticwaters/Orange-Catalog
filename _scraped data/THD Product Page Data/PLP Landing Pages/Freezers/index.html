About Freezers

Freezers are useful and long-lasting home appliances. Freezers come in two main styles, upright and chest, with many different features. When shopping for the best freezers to fit your home, think about where you'll put your freezers and how much frozen food you need to store.

The Right Freezers for Your Family

Save time and money with the right freezers for your family. Deep freezers offer you additional space to stock up on all the food items your family loves. More space now means fewer trips to the grocery store later. How do you choose the right deep freezer? Start by determining how much space you have and how much food you want to store.

Chest freezers are the most common type of freezer. These freezers are available in many sizes, from five to 22 cubic feet, and their design lets you take advantage of every inch of it. Chest freezers have many benefits. Besides being budget friendly, chest freezers are a good choice for large families because they hold more food per square foot. These freezers provide more space for your favorite frozen foods, snacks, meats and anything else you buy in bulk. Its lift-up lid allows it to close tightly and securely, so cold air doesnt escape. If youre considering a chest freezer with lock, we carry a nice selection. Chest freezers are more energy efficient but take up more floor space than upright freezers. Most chest freezers require manual defrosting. Frost-free freezers cost slightly more, but you get the convenience of never worrying about ice buildup and defrosting. If youre looking to get a manual defrost upright freezer, we have those freezers also.

Upright freezers are just like a refrigerator, and you guessed it they stand upright. So, youre taking up less floor space. These standup freezers are perfect for small spaces. Another advantage of upright freezers is you get additional storage in the door. Available adjustable shelving and removable bins help you keep foods organized. With upright freezers, food is kept in sight and easily accessible. So, you dont have to bend over or move things around to find what youre looking for. While upright freezers may cost more upfront than chest freezers, customers like that their upright freezers come in more colors and can be easily matched to their current appliances. From an upright frost free freezer to an upright freezer with drawers, youll find the upright freezers you need for your home.

Top Features of Freezers

Consider the many features and options available in todays deep freezers. Convenient LED lighting helps you locate items quickly. Removable dividers and sliding baskets keep foods organized and easy to access. Look for freezers with external temperature control to adjust the temperature easily and conveniently, without opening the lid. Some freezers also have a temperature alarm feature that alerts you if the temperature rises unexpectedly.

If you have children, make sure to keep them away from freezers. Many chest and upright freezers are outfitted with safety locks to give you additional peace of mind. And, if you plan to put any freezers in the garage, look for signage that says your freezer is garage ready. That means the freezers compressor is designed to adjust accordingly, whether your garage gets very hot or very cold.

More Types of Freezers

We have a large selection of freezers: large freezers, small freezers, residential and commercial freezers. If youre short on space, consider a space-saving portable freezer. These freezers are perfect for the basement, office or mancave. Keep your beverage of choice chilled to the perfect temperature with a convenient beverage cooler. They take up minimal space and can hold from 12 to more than 100 bottles or canned drinks.

Restaurants may require a large commercial freezer. These upright freezers offer substantial storage space, up to 72 cubic feet or more, and are constructed with sturdy, solid doors that help it stand up to the demands of the food service industry. If youre looking for a commercial freezer with glass door, we carry those freezers as well. Plus, shop our selection of medical freezers. These freezers are designed to operate at colder temperatures than standard residential freezers, to keep vaccines and other materials at safe temperatures.
