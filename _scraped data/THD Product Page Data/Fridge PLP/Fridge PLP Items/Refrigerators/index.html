About Refrigerators

When it comes to choosing refrigerators, there are three main types: top-freezer fridges, side-by-side fridges and French door fridges. Our wide range of fridges will help you take care of your family while making your kitchen shine. With our vast selection of refrigerators, we can help you find the right fridges in no time.

Choosing the Right Size Refrigerators

Make sure to consider your counter depth when looking at refrigerators. Its a common mistake, that if avoided, will save you time and money. Before you choose your refrigerators, make sure the refrigerators you ultimately select will fit in your kitchen. Be sure to measure the width, depth and height of the area where the refrigerator will go. This is especially important for apartment refrigerators, large refrigerators, wine coolers, beverage coolers, kegerators, counter depth refrigerators, mini fridges and compact refrigerators.

Counter-depth refrigerators have bodies that fit flush with cabinets and countertops. The doors on these refrigerators must extend to leave room for hinges to operate without blockage. Counter-depth refrigerators are a great choice if youre looking to upgrade a kitchen. With less of the refrigerator sticking out into the kitchen, these refrigerators offer a sleek, modern look that can easily match any existing decor. Counter-depth refrigerators come in both top freezer and bottom freezer refrigerators. Youll also want to take a look at our built-in refrigerators, panel ready refrigerators and 4 door refrigerators.

Refrigerators & Style

French door refrigerators provide wide shelves and full-width drawers, keeping your food eye-level so you dont have to bend down. French door refrigerators also save energy because you only have to open one door. These refrigerators also feature more storage capacity than some other refrigerators.

Side-by-side refrigerators offer similar benefits and provide easy access to filtered water and ice. These styles of refrigerators also feature large capacity and space. Additionally, side-by-side refrigerators offer flexible, spill-proof shelving for better storage and less maintenance.

If youre looking for a more classic style, top freezer refrigerators offer affordability and feature lots of space. Top freezer refrigerators come in a variety of sizes, styles, colors and finishes. These refrigerators also offer adjustable shelves and door bins for organization and storage, as well as humidity-controlled drawers to keep fruits and vegetables fresher longer. Worried about refrigerators sticking out beyond your counters? Consider counter depth refrigerators. If you want more space, drawer refrigerators are a great option for additional storage room. Also, our wide selection of retro refrigerators offer a sense of nostalgia. If youre hosting guests, retro refrigerators make great conversation pieces.

Refrigerators & Finishes

Once youve looked at refrigerators and picked one, its time to select a finish. There are plenty of fridges to select from that will complement the rest of your kitchen. Choose from classic white refrigerators or black refrigerators, stainless steel refrigerators, black stainless steel refrigerators, slate refrigerators or black slate refrigerators. Stainless steel refrigerators boast a timeless, elegant appearance and are easy to clean. Built-in refrigerators let you customize your kitchens appearance. These refrigerators feature contoured doors and handles that offer a sleek, sophisticated look.

Innovative Refrigerators

Purchasing refrigerators is not like it used to be. Cutting-edge smart fridges now offer Wi-Fi enabled touchscreens built into the doors, Keurig K-Cup brewing systems, hot water scheduling and temperature control features. Some refrigerators can even allow you to stream music.

Commercial Refrigerators & Freezers

If you are looking for refrigerators for a business instead of a home, you will need commercial refrigerators and commercial freezers that are up to the task at a commercial kitchen. We offer a wide variety of commercial refrigerators you can choose from to suit the needs of your business including reach-in refrigerators, merchandise refrigerators, undercounter refrigerators, and upright freezers.

Getting Refrigerators Delivered & Installed

We are your go-to destination for all refrigerators, including refrigerators with ice makers and refrigerator parts and accessories. When looking at refrigerators to purchase, think about installation. Consider the width doorway refrigerators will have to pass through when installed. In addition to checking dimensions for refrigerators, always make sure you have enough room for doors and drawers to open fully. Once youve decided on the best refrigerators for your home, we can deliver and install these refrigerators with our refrigerator delivery and installation service.
