All About Designer Bathrooms

Bathroom Vanities

Bath vanities can be much more than just a home for your sink and a place for storage. The ideal bathroom vanity sets the tone for the room and is a jumping-off point for the rest of your bathroom decor. Match your bathroom accessories with your faucet and the cabinet hardware, add vanity lighting with a similar look and choose a wall color that complements the color of your vanity. You'll find modern, rustic, traditional and more decorating styles in our diverse selection of bathroom essentials. Our vanity sale is the perfect time to upgrade your bathroom vanity with bathroom fixtures to match.

Find the Perfect Bathtub or Shower

When you think of the perfect bathtub, it should be a piece that brings you superior relaxation. Bathtubs should coordinate with the design of your entire bathroom space, but practicalities are of vital importance as well. What size and shape will fit in your space? Soaking bathtubs or whirlpool bathtubs are a great idea for people who worship their bath time. Are you looking for any special features? Are there any accessibility considerations you should make? We have everything you need as part of our bathtub sale.

If your wash time depends more on your mood, go for one of our tubs and shower combos. You can refresh your existing showers or do a total upgrade. No matter what you decide, we have plenty of walk-in shower ideas and bathtub remodeling ideas to inspire you. With big projects like bathtub or shower installations, consider our bathroom remodeling services to get started. Our shower head sale will help complete your dream bathroom.

Bathroom Fixtures

Your bathroom fixtures help set the tone of your entire bathroom. When choosing from our wide variety of bathroom sinks, bathroom faucets and shower faucets, there are a few things to consider. Choose your finish with options such as matte black, brushed nickel, chrome, brass and more. There are widespread or centerset faucets, or even single-handle faucets. You can go the extra mile in luxury when you upgrade to waterfall faucets or touchless faucets.

You want something thats functional but also stylish. Between teeth brushing and multiple hand washes, bathroom sinks and faucets get quite a bit of use every day. Choose faucets that are not only reliable and water saving, but also fit your style and budget. Shop our large selection of bathroom faucets, available in a variety of styles and finishes, in all the top brands. No matter what style or function youre looking for, we have all the bathroom essentials for you.

Choosing the Right Toilet

Of course, the toilet is essential to any bathroom. In our selection, you'll find WaterSense qualified toilets with a powerful flush, bringing efficiency without sacrificing luxury. We also offer a range of bidets. If you've ever encountered a bidet while traveling, you know they offer a practical and gentle solution for personal cleanliness while conserving paper and water. Now is a great time to look at our bidet sale and look at toilets on sale.

We Can Help

The Home Depot will help you select the perfect items for your bathroom. Our knowledgeable associates are glad to answer all your questions and offer advice to turn that bathroom into a relaxing, stress-free room. From bath vanities to bath faucets, all the way down to medicine cabinets and bath storage, we have everything you need to upgrade your bathroom your way.

Whether you want a full remodel with new waterproof flooring and an updated bathtub or a small upgrade with vanity lighting swaps or a stylish new mirror, we have something to make you smile and make the process easy. View our bathroom mirror sale or shop our Home Depot Bathroom Sale full of bathroom essentials, bathroom remodeling services and bathroom remodeling supplies to get started today.
