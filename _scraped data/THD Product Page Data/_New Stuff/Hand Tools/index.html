Tools for every project

Empower Your Tools Do more with your power tools, with the right power tool accessories from The Home Depot. Whether youre looking for saw blades, drill bits, or abrasives we have what you need. Plus, shop our large selection of power tool batteries and chargers to keep you going until the job is done.

Drill Accessories Most of your handheld power drills will come with basic drill bits, but you might consider investing in a good drill bit set. Many sets offer a variety of drill bits for drilling into wood, plastic and metal. We also have a variety of drill attachments, like right angle drill attachments that help you get into those tight spaces.

Saw Accessories There are all types of saws, but most would agree that your saw is only as good as its blade. Make sure you have the right blades, like miter saw blades for cutting moulding or trim and jigsaw blades if you need to cut curves. Circular saw blades will give you those straight-line cuts. Youll also find band saw blades, scroll saw blades, reciprocating blades, diamond saw blades, and table saw blades.

Woodworking Accessories Working with wood? From router bits to wood glue, we have the woodworking accessories you need to get the job done. If you need a little help getting started on your woodworking project, check out our Selecting the Right Router Bits guide.

Abrasives The right abrasives will give wood projects the smooth, finished look you desire. Abrasives include things like your sanding discs and polishing wheels, right down to the sandpaper and steel wool. While hand-sanding will work for some jobs, others require you to choose a power sander or belt sander. Many of these tools are available for rent at your local Home Depot store.

Retool Your Tool Box The Home Depot is your one-stop shop for power tool accessories from grinding wheels and saw tracks to power tool chargers. We carry top brands such as Ryobi, Milwaukee, Makita, Bosch, Rigid, and Diablo. Never run out of the things you need with our subscription service. Just look for the Subscribe and get 5% off every order option when you shop on homedepot.com. We have tons of subscription-eligible power tool accessories for you to choose.

Taps & Dies Ratchet up your projects with the right taps and dies from the Home Depot. Whether you need a single die cutter or a full tap and die set, we have everything you need. Additionally, shop our selection of pipe taps to stay on target at every job.

Tool Accessories Most projects have many moving parts. From corner clamps to whetstones, we have the tool accessories you need to get the job done. We offer accessories for all types of hand tools as well as cleaners, sharpeners and replacements so you can do more.

Specialty Hand Tools Do more with specialty hand tools from The Home Depot. Whether you need hand seamers, laser temperature guns or a magnetic clean sweep we have what you need. Plus, shop our large selection of clamps to keep your work area secure.

Screwdrivers & Nut Drivers Nearly every project needs a screwdriver. Take them all on with our large selection of screwdrivers and nut drivers at The Home Depot. Whether youre looking for a specialty screwdriver or a set to drive any size screw we have what you need.
