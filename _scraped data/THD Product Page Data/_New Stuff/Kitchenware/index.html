All About Kitchenware

The kitchen is the heart of every home. Make sure you have the right cookware, kitchen tools and houseware on hand so you're ready for any meal. Kitchenware and houseware make up all the things you use in your kitchen and around your home every day, from pots and pans to knives and small appliances. Whether youre a frequent entertainer looking to wow at your next dinner party, or youve just moved into your first apartment and youre stocking up on home essentials - youll find all the kitchenware and houseware you need at The Home Depot. Shop our large selection of kitchen and home essentials to fit every style, budget and skill level.

Cookware

A great place to start when shopping for new kitchen supplies is the cookware. This includes pots and pans, griddles woks and more. These items are likely to be used frequently so its important to invest in pieces that you love. We carry everything from copper and non-stick pans to brightly colored enamel stock pots, so youre sure to find a cookware set suited to your taste. Our extensive assortment also includes handy kitchen helpers like stovetop pressure cookers and Dutch ovens.

Bakeware

While cookware is generally used on the stovetop, bakeware can be put into a hot oven for roasting, broiling and, of course, baking. Quality bakeware allows you to whip up much more than pies, cookies and cakes. A good baking sheet is the perfect surface for roasting vegetables, for example, and casserole dishes can be used to bake all kinds of one-pan meals. Plus, most avid bakers are going to need sturdy muffin pans, loaf tins and cookie cutters at their disposal. Like with our cookware, our bakeware selection features many non-stick options. Dont forget new oven mitts and pot holders to protect you from high baking temperatures.

Cutlery & Tools

No kitchen is complete without quality flatware and a sharp set of chefs knives. In addition to these bare necessities, you may also want to complement your cutlery collection with cooking tools such as spatulas, whisks and tongs. Youll need colanders for washing fruit and making pasta dishes, plus salt and pepper mills to add a flavorful finish. Youre sure to serve up that next delicious meal quite a bit faster with the right kitchen tools on hand.

Small Appliances

Small kitchen appliances and electronic cooking gadgets can fall under the umbrella of kitchenware as well. Serious home chefs may require a countertop blender and a reliable hand mixer for their kitchen experiments. For those determined to measure ingredients accurately, nothing beats a digital kitchen scale. Other appliances like coffee makers, toasters, toaster ovens and electric kettles can act as the perfect finishing touch to a kitchenette or shared kitchen space. Still not sure where to start? Think about your favorite meals and the tools you would need to make them at home. Maybe youre shopping for pasta makers for Italian night or the perfect ladle for hearty soups and stews. Whatever you crave, The Home Depot has kitchenware, houseware and cooking essentials to help you pull off the perfect meal.
